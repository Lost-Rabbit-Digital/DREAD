[h1]D.R.E.A.D. - Dynamic Randomized Enemy Area Distribution[/h1]

[b]Every raid is different. Every corner is a threat.[/b]

DREAD is a comprehensive spawn overhaul mod that transforms Zero Sievert's enemy encounters with new spawn points, dynamic enemy variety, and challenging late-game features.

[h2]Features[/h2]

[h3]Enhanced Spawn Distribution[/h3]
[list]
[*] New spawn points in traditionally empty areas
[*] Variable squad sizes and compositions
[*] Per-zone spawn density configuration
[/list]

[h3]SAIN-Inspired Enemy Archetypes[/h3]
Enemies spawn with varied equipment and stats:
[list]
[*] [b]GigaChad (5%)[/b] - Elite enemies, 150% HP, +30% accuracy, master-tier gear
[*] [b]Chad (15%)[/b] - Well-equipped veterans, 120% HP, +15% accuracy
[*] [b]Normal (40%)[/b] - Standard enemies with baseline stats
[*] [b]Rat (25%)[/b] - Sneaky but poorly equipped, 80% HP
[*] [b]Timmy (15%)[/b] - Inexperienced with minimal gear, 60% HP
[/list]

[h3]Squad Spawning[/h3]
[list]
[*] Groups of 2-5 enemies spawn together as cohesive units
[*] 30% chance for squad to have a Chad/GigaChad leader
[*] Configurable squad sizes and leader probability
[/list]

[h3]Invasion Waves[/h3]
Timed enemy waves add pressure during raids:
[list]
[*] First wave: 2 minutes after raid start
[*] Subsequent waves: Every 3 minutes
[*] Up to 3 waves per raid (configurable)
[/list]

[h3]Chaos Mode (Optional)[/h3]
Cross-zone enemy variety with weighted rarity:
[list]
[*] Native (60%) - Normal zone enemies
[*] Uncommon (25%) - Adjacent zone enemies
[*] Rare (12%) - Distant zone enemies
[*] Anomalous (3%) - Late-game threats appearing early!
[/list]

[h2]Configuration[/h2]

DREAD is fully configurable via [b]dread_config.json[/b]. Customize:
[list]
[*] Enable/disable individual features
[*] Spawn density per zone (sparse/normal/dense/extreme)
[*] Archetype spawn weights
[*] Squad sizes and leader chances
[*] Invasion wave timing
[*] Difficulty presets (Tourist, Normal, Hardcore, Nightmare)
[/list]

[h2]Current Zone Support[/h2]
[list]
[*] Forest - Fully configured with 18 spawn points + boss encounters
[*] Other zones - Template spawns (community coordinates welcome!)
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*] Works with vanilla Zero Sievert
[*] Should be compatible with most other mods
[*] Does not modify core game files
[/list]

[h2]Installation[/h2]
Subscribe to install automatically. The mod activates on raid start.

[/list]
